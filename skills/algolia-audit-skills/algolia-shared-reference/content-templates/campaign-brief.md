# Campaign / Creative Brief Template

## Campaign Brief Structure

### 1. Campaign Overview
| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| Campaign name | [Name] |
| Owner | [Team/Person] |
| Start date | [Date] |
| End date | [Date] |
| Budget | [Amount/Range] |

### 2. Objective
- What is the business goal? (e.g., pipeline generation, brand awareness, product adoption)
- What does success look like? Define 2-3 KPIs with targets.

### 3. Target Audience
- **Primary ICP**: [Title, industry, company size]
- **Pain points**: [What keeps them up at night?]
- **Decision criteria**: [What matters in their evaluation?]
- **Channels they use**: [Where do they consume content?]

### 4. Key Message
- **Core message** (1 sentence): What is the single most important thing we want the audience to believe/know/do?
- **Supporting messages** (3-4 bullets): What proof points support the core message?
- **Tone**: [Refer to voice-and-tone.md for tone selection based on audience]

### 5. Deliverables
| Asset | Owner | Due Date | Status |
|-------|-------|----------|--------|
| Landing page | [Name] | [Date] | [Status] |
| Email sequence | [Name] | [Date] | [Status] |
| Social posts | [Name] | [Date] | [Status] |
| Blog post | [Name] | [Date] | [Status] |
| Sales enablement | [Name] | [Date] | [Status] |

### 6. Distribution Channels
- Paid: [Channels + budget allocation]
- Owned: [Blog, email, social, website]
- Earned: [PR, partnerships, influencers]

### 7. Brand Guardrails
- All assets must follow Algolia Brand Guidelines (this skill)
- Approved company descriptions from `messaging-framework.md`
- Visual assets from `visual-identity/` only
- Copy reviewed against `editorial-standards.md`

### 8. Measurement
- KPIs and targets defined in Section 2
- Reporting cadence: [Weekly/Bi-weekly/Monthly]
- Tools: [Analytics platforms]
