# Partner & Co-Marketing Content Guidelines

## Co-Branding Rules

### Logo Placement
- Algolia logo and partner logo should have **equal visual weight**
- Maintain appropriate spacing between logos
- Use the logo variation that works best on the chosen background
- Follow both Algolia's and the partner's brand guidelines for logo usage

### Content Principles
- **Mutual value**: Always highlight the combined value of the partnership
- **Neutral tone**: Don't favor one partner over the other
- **Respect partner guidelines**: Ask for and follow their brand requirements
- **Joint approval**: All co-branded content should be approved by both parties

## Co-Marketing Content Types

### Joint Blog Post
- Co-authored by representatives from both companies
- Highlight the integration or joint solution
- Include perspectives from both companies
- CTA can point to both companies' resources

### Integration Page
- Headline: "[Partner] + Algolia: [Combined Value Proposition]"
- Overview of the integration
- Key benefits (3-4)
- How it works (step-by-step or diagram)
- Customer example if available
- CTAs for both companies

### Co-Branded Webinar
- Joint presenters from both companies
- Agenda covers both perspectives
- Shared follow-up with resources from both companies
- Recording hosted on both companies' platforms

### Partner One-Pager
- Follow the one-pager template but include:
  - Both logos in the header
  - Joint value proposition
  - Integration capabilities
  - Combined customer proof points

## Partner Description Templates

When describing the partnership in Algolia materials:

```
Algolia integrates with [Partner] to help businesses [combined outcome].
By combining Algolia's [specific capability] with [Partner]'s [specific capability],
teams can [specific benefit].
```

## What NOT to Do
- Don't modify the partner's logo in any way
- Don't make claims about the partner's product without their approval
- Don't use the partner's brand colors for Algolia content (or vice versa)
- Don't imply endorsement beyond the scope of the partnership
