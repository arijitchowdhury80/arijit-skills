# Email Templates

## Marketing Emails

### Newsletter Structure
1. **Subject line**: 40-60 characters, sentence case, no period
2. **Preview text**: 80-100 characters, complements subject
3. **Header**: Algolia logo (Xenon variant on white)
4. **Feature story**: Headline + 2-3 paragraphs + CTA
5. **Secondary stories**: 2-3 cards with image + headline + 1-sentence summary
6. **Footer**: Social links, unsubscribe, legal

### Product Announcement Email
1. **Subject line**: "[Product Name]: [Key Benefit]"
2. **Hero**: Product screenshot/illustration + headline
3. **What's new**: 3-4 bullet points of key capabilities
4. **Why it matters**: 1 paragraph connecting to customer value
5. **CTA**: "Try it now" / "Learn more" / "Watch the demo"

### Event Invitation Email
1. **Subject line**: "Join us: [Event name] — [Date]"
2. **Hero**: Event visual
3. **Details**: What, when, where, who's speaking
4. **Value prop**: Why attend — 3 bullet points
5. **CTA**: "Register now"

## Sales Outreach Emails

### Cold Outreach Template
```
Subject: [Specific pain point relevant to their industry]

Hi [First Name],

[1 sentence referencing something specific about their company/role].

At Algolia, we help [their industry] companies like [relevant customer example]
[achieve specific outcome — e.g., "reduce search abandonment by 30%"].

[1-2 sentences on how, connected to their likely challenge].

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to explore if this could work for [Company]?

Best,
[Your name]
```

### Follow-Up Template
```
Subject: Re: [Original subject]

Hi [First Name],

Quick follow-up on my previous note. [1 sentence with new value — different
angle, new case study, new metric].

[1 sentence connecting to their specific context].

Happy to share more details over a brief call — does [day] work?

Best,
[Your name]
```

### Post-Demo Follow-Up
```
Subject: Next steps after our conversation

Hi [First Name],

Thanks for taking the time to explore Algolia. Here's a quick summary of
what we covered:

- [Key point 1 discussed]
- [Key point 2 discussed]
- [Key point 3 discussed]

As we discussed, [1 sentence on the specific opportunity/challenge they shared].

[Relevant resource — case study, documentation link, or trial link].

What's the best next step on your end?

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Email Style Rules
- **Tone**: Personal, value-driven, respectful of the reader's time
- **Length**: Marketing emails: 200-400 words. Sales emails: 75-150 words.
- **Subject lines**: Sentence case, no periods, no ALL CAPS, no excessive punctuation
- **CTA**: One primary CTA per email. Make it obvious and action-oriented.
- **Algolia references**: Follow brand terminology guidelines
- **No attachments in cold outreach** — link to resources instead
- **American English** for all marketing emails
