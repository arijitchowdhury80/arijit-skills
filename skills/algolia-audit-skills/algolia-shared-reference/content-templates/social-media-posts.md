# Social Media Post Templates

## General Rules (All Channels)
- Tag **@Algolia** and use **#Algolia** when posting about Algolia
- Post in the language of the channel (English for global channels)
- Never share confidential information
- All claims must be substantiated
- Never criticize competitors
- All images must be licensed or original Algolia assets

## LinkedIn

### Thought Leadership Post
```
[Hook — provocative question or surprising stat]

[2-3 sentences expanding the insight]

[1-2 sentences connecting to Algolia's perspective or solution]

[CTA — question to drive engagement, link to content]

#Algolia #[RelevantHashtag] #[IndustryHashtag]
```

### Product Announcement
```
[Exciting news emoji-free announcement — what's new]

[2-3 sentences on why it matters and who benefits]

[Key stats or capabilities — bullet points]

[Link to learn more]

#Algolia #[ProductHashtag]
```

### Customer Story
```
[Customer result — lead with the metric]

[1-2 sentences on how they achieved it with Algolia]

[Brief customer quote if available]

Read the full story: [link]

#Algolia #CustomerSuccess
```

### Character limits
- Post text: Up to 3,000 characters (recommended: 150-300 for engagement)
- Image: 1200 x 627 px (recommended)
