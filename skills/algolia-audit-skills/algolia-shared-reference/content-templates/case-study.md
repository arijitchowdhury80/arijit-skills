# Case Study Template

Use one of these pre-approved case-study inserts at the beginning or end of every case study:

Algolia is the market leader in AI Search and Retrieval platform that helps businesses deliver fast, relevant, and intelligent digital experiences. Powering 1.75 trillion searches a year for over 18,000 companies, Algolia enables enterprises to build unified agentic AI, generative AI, and AI Search experiences across their digital surfaces. **Note:** Adapt the description to be relevant to the vendor/context. Contact the Content Marketing Team for help adapting for specific vendors.

## Case Study Structure

### 1. Title
- Format: "How [Customer] achieved [Result] with Algolia"
- Sentence case
- Include the customer name and quantifiable result

### 2. Customer Snapshot (Sidebar/Header)
| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| Company | [Customer name] |
| Industry | [Industry] |
| Use case | [Primary use case] |
| Key results | [2-3 bullet points with numbers] |

### 3. The Challenge (1-2 paragraphs)
- What problem was the customer facing?
- What was the business impact of the problem?
- Why were existing solutions insufficient?

### 4. The Solution (2-3 paragraphs)
- How did Algolia address the challenge?
- What specific Algolia features/products were used?
- How was the implementation approached?

### 5. The Results (1-2 paragraphs + metrics)
- Quantifiable outcomes (conversion rate, search speed, revenue, etc.)
- Use specific numbers whenever possible
- Include a customer quote if available

### 6. Customer Quote
- Direct quote from a named stakeholder
- Include title and company
- The quote should validate the results or praise the partnership

### 7. Algolia Company Description
- Use one of the approved inserts above

## Style Rules for Case Studies
- **Tone**: Results-driven, specific, credible
- **Length**: 800-1,200 words
- **Always include quantifiable metrics**
- **Never make unsubstantiated claims**
- **Use the customer's voice** — include direct quotes
- **Capitalize Algolia product names** used in the solution
