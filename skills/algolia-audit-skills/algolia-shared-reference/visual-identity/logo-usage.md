# Algolia Logo Usage Guidelines

## About the Logo

The Algolia logo is a visual shorthand for what we believe in. It represents the impact we have on every website or app that uses the Algolia search and discovery platform. The logo is created using Neon Blue — a bold, vibrant color that adds to the idea of speed.

In some instances, it will not always be possible to use Algolia's blue logo. Use this guide to help make the best decision on which variation to use.

## Logo Variations

### 1. Full Xenon Variant (Preferred)
- **Blue logo mark + dark "algolia" text**
- This is the **preferred version** — your go-to choice for light backgrounds
- Use on white or light-colored backgrounds

### 2. White Text Variant
- **Blue logo mark + white "algolia" text**
- Use when working on a **dark background**
- Ensures legibility of the company name

### 3. Full White Variant
- **White logo mark + white "algolia" text**
- Use when on a **blue or photographic background**
- Always make sure the name is legible

## Logo Containers

- The logo is **always preferred without a container**
- In some exceptional cases, a container might be needed
- You can **only** use the logos in containers provided in the official logo pack
- Container shapes: rounded square containers in blue or dark variants

## Logo Guidance — What NOT to Do

### Don't Colorize
- Don't colorize the logo outside of the official possibilities listed above

### Don't Distort
- Don't rotate, distort, stretch, or skew the logo in any way
- Respect and retain the logo's aspect ratio

### No Old Logo
- Be careful to always use the latest version of the logo

### Contrast Check
- Don't place the logo on a poorly contrasted background

### Don't Modify
- Don't modify the logo in any way, such as adding effects or changing the design

## Algolia and Partners (Co-Branding)

When displaying the Algolia logo alongside partner logos:
- Maintain equal visual weight between logos
- Use appropriate spacing between logos
- Follow partner's brand guidelines for their logo
- Use the logo variation that works best with the partner's logo background
- When in doubt, use the Full Xenon Variant on a light background

## Logo Assets

Official logo files are available in the `assets/` directory and in the Algolia Logo Pack 2022:
- SVG format (preferred for web)
- PNG format (for presentations and documents)
- Available in all three color variations
- Container versions included

## Clear Space

- Always maintain adequate clear space around the logo
- Minimum clear space = the height of the "@" symbol in the logo mark
- Never crowd the logo with other elements

## Minimum Size

- Digital: minimum width of 80px
- Print: minimum width of 20mm
- The logo mark alone can be used at smaller sizes where the full logo would be illegible
