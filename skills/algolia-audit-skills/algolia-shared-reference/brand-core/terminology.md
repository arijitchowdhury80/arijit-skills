## Algolia References

- It's **OK** to use Algolia as a noun: "After implementing Algolia..."
- Algolia can be used as an adjective: "the Algolia platform," "Algolia's API"

## General Terminology Guidelines

### Abbreviations
- **Ampersand (&)**: Avoid unless space is tight
  - Correct: "Search and Discovery"
  - Acceptable (space-constrained): "Search & Discovery"
- If an ampersand must be used in a phrase that would normally include a serial comma, delete the comma
  - Correct: "Search, Discovery & Recommendations"

### Acronyms
- If well-known in the industry (API, HTML, CSS, SDK, REST, JSON), don't spell it out
- If there's a chance readers won't recognize it, spell it out on first reference, then use the acronym
  - Example: "Coordinated Universal Time (UTC)"
- Common Algolia-context acronyms that DON'T need spelling out:
  - API, SDK, REST, JSON, HTML, CSS, AI, ML, RAG, LLM, NLP, SEO, SaaS, UI, UX, CDN, DNS

### Capitalization Rules

**Use sentence case** for:
- Titles, headings, and subheadings (capitalize only the first letter of the first word)
- Form titles and names of fields
- Menu items

**Use title case** for:
- Menu names
- Button names

**Capitalize:**
- Proper nouns (names of products, people, companies, countries)
- People's titles BEFORE their name: "Chief Information Officer Dave Christy"
- Names of departments/teams (but not the words "department" or "team"): "Marketing team"
- First word of list items
- Algolia product and feature names (see list above)
- "Search and Discovery" as a branded phrase

**Don't capitalize:**
- People's titles AFTER their name: "Dave Christy, chief information officer"
- The first word after a colon (:) unless it's a proper noun
- The words "search" or "discovery" when used as general terms

### Words and Phrases to Avoid

| Avoid | Use Instead |
|-------|-------------|
| "Algolia thinks..." | "Algolia enables..." or specific capability |
| "Brands are looking at..." | "Business leaders are exploring..." |
| "Semantic search looks to change..." | "Semantic search is changing..." |
| "Leverage" | "Use" or "apply" |
| "Synergy" | "Collaboration" or "combined value" |
| "Best-in-class" (without proof) | Specific metric or analyst recognition |
| "World-class" (without proof) | Specific achievement or recognition |
| "Solution" (overused) | "Platform," "product," "approach" |
| "Utilize" | "Use" |
| "In order to" | "To" |
| "Due to the fact that" | "Because" |
| "At the end of the day" | Cut entirely |
