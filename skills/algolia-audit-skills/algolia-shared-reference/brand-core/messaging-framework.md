### Company Profile (Full — for case studies, about pages, press kits)
Algolia is the leading Retrieval AI platform, powering more than 1.75 trillion searches a year for over 18,000 businesses and millions of developers worldwide. Built on a developer-first, API-first foundation, Algolia enables teams to combine keyword, vector, and hybrid retrieval with advanced rules, personalization, and analytics to deliver the world's fastest and most scalable AI-powered search and discovery experiences.

At its core, Algolia helps businesses surface the right information instantly and at scale — across web, mobile, and emerging AI-driven interfaces. Its Retrieval AI platform serves as the foundation for modern intelligent applications, supporting everything from traditional search and discovery to Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) and LLM-powered experiences.

Backed by more than a decade of innovation, Algolia is redefining how companies and builders create agentic, retrieval-powered applications. Through its MCP server and next-generation products such as NuFact and Agent Studio, Algolia enables developers to embed advanced, high-performance retrieval directly into AI workflows and agents — bridging the gap between search, data, and action.

Algolia processes over 50 trillion searches every week and is trusted by brands operating at global scale. The platform is designed to ensure reliability, performance, and scalability — handling peak demand without disruption, making it a critical infrastructure layer for high-traffic digital experiences.

Named a Leader in the 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Search and Product Discovery, recognized for its depth of functionality, ability to support diverse use cases, and continuous innovation across hybrid and vector retrieval, GenAI, and agentic capabilities. Algolia was also named a Leader in the IDC MarketScape covering Search and Product Discovery.
